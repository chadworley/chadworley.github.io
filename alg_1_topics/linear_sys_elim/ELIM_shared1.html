  1. Question

    Solve the system. \[\begin{align}{3}x+{9}y&=60\\ {3}x+{4}y&=40\end{align}\]

    • \(x=\)
    • \(y=\)


    Solution

    Subtract the 2nd equation from the 1st equation.

    \[{5}y=20\]

    Divide both sides by 5.

    \[y=4\]

    Plug in \(y=4\) into 1st equation. \[{3}x+9\cdot4=60\]

    Simplify the product.

    \[{3}x+36=60\]

    Subtract 36 from both sides.

    \[{3}x=24\]

    So,

    \[x=8\]
