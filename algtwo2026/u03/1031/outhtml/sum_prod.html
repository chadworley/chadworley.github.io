  1. Question

    Find two numbers that have:

    • a sum (result of addition) of -11
    • and a product (result of multiplication) of 28

    1. -10
    2. -9
    3. -8
    4. -7
    5. -6
    6. -5
    7. -4
    8. -3
    9. -2
    10. -1
    11. 0
    12. 1
    13. 2
    14. 3
    15. 4
    16. 5
    17. 6
    18. 7
    19. 8
    20. 9
    21. 10

    Solution

    Find all the factor pairs of 28. Then, determine which pair has a sum of -11.

    product sum
    (1)*(28) = 28 (1)+(28) = 29
    (-1)*(-28) = 28 (-1)+(-28) = -29
    (2)*(14) = 28 (2)+(14) = 16
    (-2)*(-14) = 28 (-2)+(-14) = -16
    (4)*(7) = 28 (4)+(7) = 11
    (-4)*(-7) = 28 (-4)+(-7) = -11

    So the two numbers that have a sum of -11 and a product of 28 are:

    • -7 and -4
