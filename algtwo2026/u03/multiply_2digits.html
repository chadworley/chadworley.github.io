Solution

\[76\cdot 48 = 3648\]

* \(70\) \(6\) total
\(40\) 2800 240 3040

total 3360 288 3648

At some point I learned about the multiplication by lines method. Youll notice there are 7 lines above 6 lines, both with positive slope. There are also 4 lines above 8 lines, both with negative slope. The number of intersections in the four corners are found with multiplication: \(7\cdot4=28\) and \(6\cdot4=24\) and \(7\cdot8=56\) and \(6\cdot8=48\).


\[2800 + 240 + 560 + 48 ~~=~~ 3648\]
