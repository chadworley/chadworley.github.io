Solution

\[96\cdot 48 = 4608\]

* \(90\) \(6\) total

total 4320 288 4608

At some point I learned about the multiplication by lines method. Youll notice there are 9 lines above 6 lines, both with positive slope. There are also 4 lines above 8 lines, both with negative slope. The number of intersections in the four corners are found with multiplication: \(9\cdot4=36\) and \(6\cdot4=24\) and \(9\cdot8=72\) and \(6\cdot8=48\).


\[3600 + 240 + 720 + 48 ~~=~~ 4608\]
