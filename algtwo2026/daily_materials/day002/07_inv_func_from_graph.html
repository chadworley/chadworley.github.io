  • If \(f(x)=3\), then find \(x\). In other words, evaluate \(f^{-1}(3)\).

Solution

Find the correct point on the graph where \(f(x)=3\).


\[f(6)=3\]

Thus,

\[f^{-1}(3) ~=~ 6\]
