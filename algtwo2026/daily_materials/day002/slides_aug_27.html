Evaluate function from algebraic definition

Let a function, \(f\), be defined with the following algebraic expression:

\[f(x)~=~2x+3\]

  • Evaluate:
    • \(f(4)~=~ ?\)
    • \(f(4) ~=~ 2\cdot 4 + 3\)
    • \(f(4) ~=~ 11\)
    • \(f(10) ~=~ ?\)
    • \(f(10) ~=~ 23\)
    • \(f(100) ~=~ ?\)
    • \(f(100) ~=~ 203\)
