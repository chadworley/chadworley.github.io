Solution

To evaluate \(f(8)\), start by finding \(x=8\) on the horizontal axis. Move up until you hit the curve (or a filled dot… NOT an empty dot…). This is marked with a red circle below. Then read off the \(y\) coordinate of the point.

\[f(8)~=~0\]

To evaluate \(f^{-1}(7)\), start by finding \(y=7\) on the vertical axis. Move sideways until you hit the curve (or a filled dot… NOT an empty dot…). This is marked with a purple circle. Then read off the \(x\) coordinate of the point.

\[f^{-1}(7)~=~6\]
