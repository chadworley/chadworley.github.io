  1. Question

    Find all solutions to the equation below:

    \[x^2~=~9\]


    1. -9
    2. -8
    3. -7
    4. -6
    5. -5
    6. -4
    7. -3
    8. -2
    9. -1
    10. 0
    11. 1
    12. 2
    13. 3
    14. 4
    15. 5
    16. 6
    17. 7
    18. 8
    19. 9

    Solution

    Notice, because the squaring function is NOT one-to-one, when we “undo” it on both sides of an equation, we need to consider multiple possibilities.

    In this case, you should know that, when squared, both \(-3\) and \(3\) produce \(9\).

    \[(-3)^2~=~9\] \[(3)^2~=~9\]

    Thus the solutions are \(-3\) and \(3\).

    You might show your work as follows:

    \[x^2 ~=~ 9\] \[x ~=~ \pm \sqrt{9}\] \[x ~=~ \pm 3\]
