  1. Question

    Three functions vary in time as shown in the table below.

    t f(t) g(t) h(t)
    30 52 54 99
    35 16 80 33
    38 34 97 97
    48 30 80 39
    49 24 85 23
    50 12 91 32
    54 9 23 89
    61 28 50 58
    78 32 12 43
    80 88 58 55
    1. Find the average rate of change of function \(f\) between \(t=48\) and \(t=54\). Express your answer as a fraction in reduced form.
    1. Find the average rate of change of function \(g\) between \(t=30\) and \(t=78\). Express your answer as a fraction in reduced form.
    1. Find the average rate of change of function \(h\) between \(t=38\) and \(t=54\). Express your answer as a fraction in reduced form.


    Solution

    1. The average rate of change of function \(f\) between \(t=48\) and \(t=54\) =

    \[\frac{f(54)-f(48)}{54-48}~=~\frac{9-30}{54-48} ~=~ \frac{-21}{6} ~=~ \frac{-7}{2}\]

    1. The average rate of change of function \(g\) between \(t=30\) and \(t=78\) =

    \[\frac{g(78)-g(30)}{78-30}~=~\frac{12-54}{78-30} ~=~ \frac{-42}{48} ~=~ \frac{-7}{8}\]

    1. The average rate of change of function \(h\) between \(t=38\) and \(t=54\) =

    \[\frac{h(54)-h(38)}{54-38}~=~\frac{89-97}{54-38} ~=~ \frac{-8}{16} ~=~ \frac{-1}{2}\]
