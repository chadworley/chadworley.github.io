  1. Question

    Let \(f(x)=\frac{x^3-3x^2+2}{2}\). Function \(g\) is made with a simple transformation (horizontal or vertical shift or stretch with possible reflection). Both functions are graphed below.


    1. Which equation was used to generate \(g\)?
    1. What is the value of \(k\)?


    Solution

    If you open Desmos and type \(f(x)=\frac{x^3-3x^2+2}{2}\) and then type

    \[g(x) = -2 \cdot f(x)\]

    Youll get a picture that looks like the given graph.

    So, the equation was \(g(x) = k \cdot f(x)\), and the parameter was \(k=-2\).
