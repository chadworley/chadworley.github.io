  1. Question

    Let functions \(f\) and \(g\) be defined by the table below.

    \(x\) \(f(x)\) \(g(x)\)
    1 8 9
    2 7 10
    3 4 6
    4 10 8
    5 1 7
    6 2 4
    7 9 3
    8 6 1
    9 3 5
    10 5 2

    Evaluate the following:

    • \(f(g(9)) =\)
    • \(g(f(9)) =\)
    • \(f(f(7)) =\)
    • \(g(g(9)) =\)
    • \(f(g(g(5))) =\)


    Solution

    • \(f(g(9))=f(5)=1\)
    • \(g(f(9))=g(3)=6\)
    • \(f(f(7))=f(9)=3\)
    • \(g(g(9))=g(5)=7\)
    • \(f(g(g(5)))=f(g(7))=f(3)=4\)
