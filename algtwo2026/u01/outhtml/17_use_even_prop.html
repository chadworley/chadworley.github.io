  1. Question

    Let \(f\) be an even function and \(f(43.3)=-58.6\). Evaluate \(f(-43.3)\).


    Solution

    Since \(f\) is even, we know \(f(-x)=f(x)\) for any value of \(x\). Thus \(f(-43.3)=f(43.3)\). So,

    \[f(-43.3)=-58.6\]

    Notice the reflective symmetry across the \(y\) axis.
