  1. Question

    Let \(f\) be an odd function and \(f(43.3)=-58.6\). Evaluate \(f(-43.3)\).


    Solution

    Since \(f\) is odd, we know \(f(-x)=-f(x)\) for any value of \(x\). Thus \(f(-43.3)=-f(43.3)\). So,

    \[f(-43.3)=58.6\]

    Notice the 180\(^{\circ}\) rotational symmetry around the origin.
