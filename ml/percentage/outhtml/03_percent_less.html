  1. Question

    What is 7.72% less than 522?


    Solution

    For a “percent less” question, the scale factor is found by dividing the percentage number by 100 and then subtracting it from 1.

    Determine that the scale factor equals 0.9228 and multiply it by the given value.

    \[0.9228\cdot 522 ~=~ 481.7016\]
