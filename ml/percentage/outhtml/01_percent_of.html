  1. Question

    What is 0.232% of 2.72?


    Solution

    For a “percent of” question, the scale factor is simply the given percentage number divided by 100.

    Determine that the scale factor equals 0.00232 and multiply it by the given value.

    \[0.00232\cdot 2.72 ~=~ 0.0063104\]
