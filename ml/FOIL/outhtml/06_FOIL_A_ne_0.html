  1. Question

    The equivalence below is true for any value of \(x\).

    \[(2x+5)(9x+3) \equiv Ax^2+Bx+C\]

    Find \(A\), \(B\), and \(C\).

    • \(A=\)
    • \(B=\)
    • \(C=\)


    Solution

    FOIL!

    \[(2x+5)(9x+3)~\equiv~(2)(9)x^2+(2)(3)x+(5)(9)x+(5)\cdot(3)\] \[(2x+5)(9x+3)~\equiv~(18)x^2+(6)x+(45)x+(15)\]

    Combine like terms and simplify.

    \[(2x+5)(9x+3)~\equiv~18x^2+51x+15\]

    So,

    \[A=18\] \[B=51\] \[C=15\]
