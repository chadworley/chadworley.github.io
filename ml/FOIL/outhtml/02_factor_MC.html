  1. Question

    Factor \(x^2-9x+18\).


    1. \((x+5)(x+2)\)
    2. \((x+1)(x+8)\)
    3. \((x+9)(x-9)\)
    4. \((x-4)(x-5)\)
    5. \((x-6)(x-3)\)
    6. \((x-3)(x+1)\)

    Solution

    You can FOIL every option.

    In short, we need two numbers that add to -9 and multiply to 18. Those numbers are -6 and -3, so:

    \[(x-6)(x-3)\]
