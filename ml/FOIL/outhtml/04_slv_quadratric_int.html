    Solution

    By guess and check, we find the two integers with sum -9 and product 18 are \(-6\) and \(-3\).

    The sum: \[(-6)+(-3) = -9\]

    The product: \[(-6)(-3) = 18\] This allows us to write the equation in factored form.

    \[\left(x - 6\right) \left(x - 3\right) = 0 \]

    The zero-product rule lets us find the solutions.

    \[x = 6\] \[x = 3\]
