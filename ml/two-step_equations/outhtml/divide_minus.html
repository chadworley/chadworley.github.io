  1. Question

    Solve for \(x\).

    \[\frac{x}{8}-2~=~5\]

    • \(x=\)

    Solution

    I’m going to first rewrite the given equation.

    \[\frac{x}{8}-2~=~5\]

    Notice the last operation (following PEMDAS, on the side with \(x\)) is subtraction by 2. Undo this by adding 2 to both sides.

    \[\frac{x}{8}~=~7\]

    Now the last operation is division by 8. Undo the addition by multiplying both sides by 8.

    \[x=56\]
