  1. Question

    Solve for \(x\).

    \[\frac{x+9}{5}~=~8\]

    • \(x=\)

    Solution

    I’m going to first rewrite the given equation.

    \[\frac{x+9}{5}~=~8\]

    Notice the last operation (following PEMDAS, on the side with \(x\)) is division by 5. Undo this by multiplying both sides by 5.

    \[x+9~=~40\]

    Now the last operation is addition by 9. Undo the addition by subtracting 9 from both sides.

    \[x~=~31\]
