    Solve for \(x\).

    \[(x+9)\cdot 8~=~40\]

    • \(x=\)

    Solution

    I’m going to first rewrite the given equation.

    \[(x+9)\cdot 8~=~40\]

    Notice the last operation (following PEMDAS, on the side with \(x\)) is multiplication by 8. Undo this by dividing both sides by 8.

    \[x+9~=~5\]

    Now the last operation is addition by 9. Undo the addition by subtracting 9 from both sides.

    \[x~=~-4\]
