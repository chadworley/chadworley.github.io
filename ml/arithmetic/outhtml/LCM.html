    What is the least common multiple of 20 and 315?


    Solution

    Determine the prime factorization for each by using a factor tree.

    \[20 = 2^{2}\cdot 5^{1}\] \[315 = 3^{2}\cdot 5^{1}\cdot 7^{1}\]

    Make a Venn diagram.

    plot of chunk unnamed-chunk-3

    Find the product of the union.

    \[2\cdot2\cdot5\cdot3\cdot3\cdot7=1260\]
