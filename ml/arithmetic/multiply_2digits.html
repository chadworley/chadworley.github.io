Solution

At some point I learned about the multiplication by lines method. You’ll notice there are 9 lines above 6 lines, both with positive slope. There are also 7 lines above 8 lines, both with negative slope. The number of intersections in the four corners are found with multiplication: \(9\cdot7=63\) and \(6\cdot7=42\) and \(9\cdot8=72\) and \(6\cdot8=48\).


\[6300 + 420 + 720 + 48 ~~=~~ 7488\]
