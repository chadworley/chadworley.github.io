  1. Question

    In a school, there are two clubs: club A and club B. Students can join one club, both clubs, or neither club.

    • The number of students in only club B (and not club A) is 5.
    • The number of students in club A is 34.
    • The number of students in both clubs (in A and B) is 8.
    • The total number of students in the school is 41.

    Calculate the following:

    • How many students are only in club A (and not club B)? students
    • How many students are in club B? students
    • How many students are in at least one club? students
    • How many students are in neither club? students


    Solution

    • The number of students in only club A (and not club B) is 26.
    • The number of students in club B is 13.
    • The number of students in at least one club is 39.
    • The number of students in neither club is 2.
