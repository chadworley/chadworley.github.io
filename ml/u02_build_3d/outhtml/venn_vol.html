  1. Question

    In SketchUp, someone created two overlapping objects. Call them object A and object B.

    • The volume remaining when object B is subtracted from object A is 26 cubic meters.
    • The volume remaining when object A is subtracted from object B is 5 cubic meters.
    • The volume of object B is 13 cubic meters.

    Calculate the following volumes:

    • What is the volume of the intersection? \(\mathrm{m^3}\)
    • What is the volume of the union? \(\mathrm{m^3}\)
    • What is the volume of object A? \(\mathrm{m^3}\)


    Solution

    • The volume of the intersection is 8 cubic meters.
    • The volume of the union is 39 cubic meters.
    • The volume of object A is 34 cubic meters.
