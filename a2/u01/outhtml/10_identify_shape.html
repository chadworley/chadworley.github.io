  1. Question

    Match the five histograms with their appropriate description.


    • Bimodal:
    • Bell:
    • Negative-skew:
    • Uniform:
    • Positive-skew:


    Solution

    This is definitional.

    • Bimodal: distribution 2
    • Bell: distribution 1
    • Negative-skew: distribution 3
    • Uniform: distribution 4
    • Positive-skew: distribution 5

    I also will say “left-skewed” for negatively skewed and “right-skewed” for positively skewed.
