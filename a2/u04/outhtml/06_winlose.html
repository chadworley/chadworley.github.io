  1. Question

    Cindy has two games today. Each game she will either win or lose. She has a 0.31 chance of winning the first game and a 0.62 chance of winning the second game.

    1. What is the chance Cindy wins both games?
    2. What is the chance Cindy loses both games?
    3. What is the chance Cindy wins once and loses once (in either order)?


    Solution

    video

    \[P(WW) = 0.31 \times 0.62 = 0.1922 \] \[P(LL) = 0.69 \times 0.38 = 0.2622 \] \[P(WL) = 0.31\times 0.38 = 0.1178 \] \[P(LW) = 0.69\times 0.62 = 0.4278\]

    • P(WW) = 0.31 * 0.62 = 0.1922
    • P(LL) = (1-0.31) * (1-0.62) = 0.2622
    • P(WL or LW) = 1-0.1922-0.2622 = 0.5456
