  1. Question

    In a school, there are two clubs: club A and club B. Students can join one club, both clubs, or neither club.

    • The total number of students in the school is 73.
    • The number of students in only club A (and not club B) is 22.
    • The number of students in club A is 29.
    • The number of students in neither club is 28.

    Calculate the following:

    • How many students are in BOTH clubs? students
    • How many students are in at least one club? students
    • How many students are only in club B (and not club A)? students
    • How many students are in club B? students


    Solution

    • The number of students in both clubs (in A and B) is 7.
    • The number of students in at least one club is 45.
    • The number of students in only club B (and not club A) is 16.
    • The number of students in club B is 23.
