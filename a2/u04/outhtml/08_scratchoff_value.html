08_scratchoff_value


  1. Question

    A scratch-off lottery ticket has the following payout probabilities:

    payout ($) probability
    0 0.35
    41 0.24
    76 0.23
    81 0.18

    What is the value, in dollars, of the unscratched ticket (to a risk-neutral individual)?


    Solution

    The expected value is the weighted average of the payouts weighted by their probabilities. The expected value is the sum of the products of each payout times its probability.

    \[\mu ~=~ \sum x\cdot P(x)\]

    \[\mu ~=~ (0\cdot0.35)+(41\cdot0.24)+(76\cdot0.23)+(81\cdot0.18)\]

    \[\mu ~=~ 0+9.84+17.48+14.58\]

    \[\mu ~=~ 41.9\]

    A risk-neutral person would value an unscratched ticket at $41.90.
