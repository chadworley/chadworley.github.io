  1. Question

    Consider the two numbers, \(a\) and \(b\), expressed below.

    \[a ~~=~~ {3}\cdot 10^{4}~+~10^{3}~+~{2}\cdot 10~+~{1}\]

    \[b ~~=~~ {8}\cdot 10^{5}~+~10^{4}~+~{8}\cdot 10^{3}~+~{7}\cdot 10^{2}~+~{2}\cdot 10~+~{2}\]

    What is the value of \(a+b\)?


    Solution

    First, you can unsimplify each:

    \[a = {0}\cdot 10^{5}~+~{3}\cdot 10^{4}~+~{1}\cdot 10^{3}~+~{0}\cdot 10^{2}~+~{2}\cdot 10^{1}~+~{1}\cdot 10^{0}\] \[b = {8}\cdot 10^{5}~+~{1}\cdot 10^{4}~+~{8}\cdot 10^{3}~+~{7}\cdot 10^{2}~+~{2}\cdot 10^{1}~+~{2}\cdot 10^{0}\] Combine the ones, tens, hundreds, thousands…

    \[a+b={8}\cdot 10^{5}~+~{4}\cdot 10^{4}~+~{9}\cdot 10^{3}~+~{7}\cdot 10^{2}~+~{4}\cdot 10^{1}~+~{3}\cdot 10^{0}\]

    \[a+b=849743\]

    3 1 0 2 1
    + 8 1 8 7 2 2
    = 8 4 9 7 4 3
