  1. Question

    The product of a linear polynomial times a cubic polynomial is a quartic polynomial. Find the product of the linear binomial and the cubic trinomial shown below.

    \[r(x)~~=~~\left({-6}x-{3}\right) \cdot \left({-9}x^{3}-{8}x^{2}+x+{4}\right)\]

    Write the resulting quartic polynomial in standard form.

    • \(r(x)~=\) \(x^4\) \(x^3\) \(x^2\) \(x\)


    Solution

    * \(-6x\) \(-3\)
    \(-9x^2\) \(54x^3\) \(-24x^2\)
    \(-8x\) \(48x^2\) \(27x\)
    \(1\) \(-6x\) \(24\)

    \[r(x)~~=~~ {54}x^{4}+{48}x^{3}-{6}x^{2}-{24}x+{27}x^{3}+{24}x^{2}-{3}x-{12}\]

    Combine like terms.

    \[r(x)~~=~~ {54}x^{4}+{75}x^{3}+{18}x^{2}-{27}x-{12}\]
