  1. Question

    The product of a quadratic polynomial times a quadratic polynomial is a quartic polynomial. Find the product of the quadratic trinomials shown below.

    \[r(x)~~=~~\left({-6}x^{2}-{3}x-{9}\right) \cdot \left({-8}x^{2}+x+{4}\right)\]

    Write the resulting quartic polynomial in standard form.

    • \(r(x)~=\) \(x^4\) \(x^3\) \(x^2\) \(x\)


    Solution

    * \(-6x\) \(-3\)
    \(-8x^2\) \(48x^3\) \(24x^2\)
    \(1x\) \(-6x^2\) \(-3x\)
    \(4\) \(-24x\) \(-12\)

    \[r(x)~~=~~ {48}x^{4}-{6}x^{3}-{24}x^{2}+{24}x^{3}-{3}x^{2}-{12}x+{72}x^{2}-{9}x-{36}\]

    Combine like terms.

    \[r(x)~~=~~ {48}x^{4}+{18}x^{3}+{45}x^{2}-{21}x-{36}\]
