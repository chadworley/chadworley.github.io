  1. Question

    Let polynomials \(p(x)\) and \(q(x)\) be defined below. \[p(x)~=~ {3}x^{10}-{3}x^{5}\] \[q(x)~=~ {3}x^{8}+{4}x^{2}\]

    The FOIL method is used to multiply two binomials. Letâ€™s visualize the FOIL method with a product table.

    * \(3x^{10}\) \(-3x^{5}\)
    \(3x^{8}\) \(x\) \(x\)
    \(4x^{2}\) \(x\) \(x\)

    Let \(r(x)\) represent the result of multiplying \(p(x)\) and \(q(x)\).

    \[r(x) ~=~ p(x)\cdot q(x)\]

    \[r(x) ~=~ \left({3}x^{10}-{3}x^{5}\right)\cdot \left({3}x^{8}+{4}x^{2}\right)\]

    Thus, we can write \(r(x)\) in standard form (highest degree to lowest degree)?

    • \(r(x)=\) \(x\)\(x\)\(x\)\(x\)


    Solution

    * \(3x^{10}\) \(-3x^{5}\)
    \(3x^{8}\) \(9x^{18}\) \(-9x^{13}\)
    \(4x^{2}\) \(12x^{12}\) \(-12x^{7}\)

    \(r(x) = {9}x^{18}-{9}x^{13}+{12}x^{12}-{12}x^{7}\)
