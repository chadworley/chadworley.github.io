Question

A circle with radius \(r=2\) centimeters is cut into 5 congruent sectors. A length of red string is pulled around the circle, and the string is exactly long enough to span the arc of 3 adjacent sectors, as shown in the diagram below.


How long, in centimeters, is the red string? Please answer as a decimal approximation accurate within \(\pm 0.01\) centimeters.

Solution

Find the diameter.

\[d=2r\] \[d=2(2)\] \[d=4\]

Find the circumference.

\[C = \pi d\] \[C = \pi (4)\] \[C~\approx~12.5663706\]
