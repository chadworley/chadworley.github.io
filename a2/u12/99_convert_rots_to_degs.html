Question

Convert 3.4 rotations to degrees. How many degrees is equivalent to 3.4 rotations?

Solution

One rotation is equivalent to 360\(^\circ\). Use this equivalence as a conversion factor.

\[3.4~\mathrm{ rotations} \cdot \frac{360^\circ}{1~\mathrm{ rotation}} ~~=~~ 1224^\circ\]
