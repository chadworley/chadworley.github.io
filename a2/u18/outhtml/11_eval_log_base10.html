  1. Question

    • \(\log_{10}(0.01)=\)

    • \(\log_{10}(1000)=\)

    • \(\log_{10}(100)=\)

    • \(\log_{10}(0.1)=\)

    • \(\log_{10}(10000)=\)



    Solution

    • \(10^{-2}=\frac{1}{100}\) therefore \(\log_{10}\left(\frac{1}{100}\right)=-2\)
    • \(10^{3}=1000\) therefore \(\log_{10}(1000)=3\)
    • \(10^{2}=100\) therefore \(\log_{10}(100)=2\)
    • \(10^{-1}=\frac{1}{10}\) therefore \(\log_{10}\left(\frac{1}{10}\right)=-1\)
    • \(10^{4}=10000\) therefore \(\log_{10}(10000)=4\)
