  1. Question

    Solve for \(x\).

    \[\log_{2}\left(x+4\right)-7=y\]


    1. \(x=2^{y-7}-4\)
    2. \(x=2^{y-4}-7\)
    3. \(x=2^{y+7}-4\)
    4. \(x=2^{y+4}-7\)
    5. \(x=2^{y/7}-4\)
    6. \(x=2^{y/4}-7\)
    7. \(x=2^{7 y}-4\)
    8. \(x=2^{4 y}-7\)
    9. \(x=2^{y-7}+4\)
    10. \(x=2^{y-4}+7\)
    11. \(x=2^{y+7}+4\)
    12. \(x=2^{y+4}+7\)
    13. \(x=2^{y/7}+4\)
    14. \(x=2^{y/4}+7\)
    15. \(x=2^{7 y}+4\)
    16. \(x=2^{4 y}+7\)
    17. \(x=\frac{2^{y-7}}{4}\)
    18. \(x=\frac{2^{y-4}}{7}\)
    19. \(x=\frac{2^{y+7}}{4}\)
    20. \(x=\frac{2^{y+4}}{7}\)
    21. \(x=\frac{2^{y/7}}{4}\)
    22. \(x=\frac{2^{y/4}}{7}\)
    23. \(x=\frac{2^{7 y}}{4}\)
    24. \(x=\frac{2^{4 y}}{7}\)
    25. \(x=4\cdot 2^{y-7}\)
    26. \(x=7\cdot 2^{y-4}\)
    27. \(x=4\cdot 2^{y+7}\)
    28. \(x=7\cdot 2^{y+4}\)
    29. \(x=4\cdot 2^{y/7}\)
    30. \(x=7\cdot 2^{y/4}\)
    31. \(x=4\cdot 2^{7 y}\)
    32. \(x=7\cdot 2^{4 y}\)

    Solution

    Rewrite the given equation.

    \[\log_{2}\left(x+4\right)-7=y\]

    As a first step, on both sides of the equation, add 7.

    \[\log_{2}\left(x+4\right) = y+7\]

    To undo the \(\log_{2}\) function, raise each side of the equation as an exponent with a base of 2.

    \[2^{\log_{2}\left(x+4\right)} = 2^{y+7}\]

    \[x+4 = 2^{y+7}\]

    As a final step, on both sides of the equation, subtract 4.

    \[x=2^{y+7}-4\]
