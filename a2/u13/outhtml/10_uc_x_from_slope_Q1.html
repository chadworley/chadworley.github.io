  1. Question

    In the first quadrant, a point is on the unit circle (centered at the origin). A line connecting the point to the origin has a slope of 0.38, as enforced by the diagram below:


    Find a decimal approximation of \(x\) accurate within \(\pm 0.01\).


    Solution

    Pythagorean Theorem!

    \[x^2+(0.38x)^2=1^2\]

    Evaluate the squares.

    \[x^2+0.1444x^2=1\]

    Combine like terms.

    \[1.1444x^2=1\]

    Divide both sides by 1.1444.

    \[x^2=\frac{1}{1.1444}\]

    Square root both sides. In this case, we are only interested in the positive square root.

    \[x=\sqrt{\frac{1}{1.1444}}\]

    Get a decimal approximation.

    \[x=0.9347836\]
