01_triangle_3rd_angle_deg


  1. Question

    A triangle is drawn. Two of the interior angle measures are known to be 72.6° and 45.5°. Find the third interior angle measure in degrees.



    Solution

    The interior angle measures sum to 180°.

    \[180-72.6-45.5~=~61.9\]
