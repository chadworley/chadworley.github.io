  1. Question

    Let function \(f\) be defined as follows: \[f(x) ~=~ - 5 x^{3} - 2 x^{2} + 5 x - 4\] Simplify \(-f(x)\).

    Your answer: \(x^3\) \(x^2\) \(x\)



    Solution

    Multiply the whole expression by \((-1)\).

    \[-f(x) ~=~ 5 x^{3} + 2 x^{2} - 5 x + 4\]

  3. Question

    Let function \(f\) be defined as follows: \[f(x) ~=~ - 3 x^{3} - 5 x^{2} + 2 x + 3\] Simplify \(f(-x)\).

    Your answer: \(x^3\) \(x^2\) \(x\)



    Solution

    Substitute \(-x\) for \(x\) and simplify.

    \[f(-x) ~=~ 3 x^{3} - 5 x^{2} - 2 x + 3\]
