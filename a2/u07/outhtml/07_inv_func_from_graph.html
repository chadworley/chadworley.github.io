07_inv_func_from_graph


  1. Question

    A function \(f\) is graphed below.


    • If \(f(x)=2\), then find \(x\). In other words, evaluate \(f^{-1}(2)\).

    Solution

    Find the correct point on the graph where \(f(x)=2\).


    \[f(4)=2\]

    Thus,

    \[f^{-1}(2) ~=~ 4\]
