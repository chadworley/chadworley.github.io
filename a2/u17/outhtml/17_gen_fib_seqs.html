  1. Question

    As stated in this wikipedia article, many variations of the Fibonacci sequence have been studied. Consider the following recursive definition:

    \[\begin{align}f(n)~~&=~~0.62\cdot f(n-2)~+~f(n-1)\\ f(1)~~&=~~0\\ f(2)~~&=~~1 \end{align}\]

    Find \(f(18)\). The tolerance is \(\pm 0.1\).


    Solution

    Type the equations into Desmos. Click Create Table. Extend the table to \(x=18\) to find \(f(18)\).

    \[f(18)=242.1280728\]
