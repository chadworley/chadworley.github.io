  1. Question


    A fractal is generated such that the biggest circle has an area of 1 square units. The next generation has 8 circles, each with an area of \(k=0.04\) square units. In the next generation of 64 circles, each has an area of \(k^2=0.0016\), then 512 circles with area \(k^3\) each, etc…

    Find the area of the fractal. The tolerance is \(\pm 0.01\).


    Solution

    We find the common ratio \(r = (8)(0.04) = 0.32\).

    \[1 + 0.32 + 0.32^2 + 0.32^3 + \ldots ~=~ \frac{1}{1-0.32} ~=~ 1.4705882\]
