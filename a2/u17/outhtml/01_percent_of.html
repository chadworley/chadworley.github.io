  1. Question

    What is 20.1% of 82?


    Solution

    For a “percent of” question, the scale factor is simply the given percentage number divided by 100.

    Determine that the scale factor equals 0.201 and multiply it by the given value.

    \[0.201\cdot 82 ~=~ 16.482\]
