  1. Question

    On Monday morning, an investor purchased a stock for $4.23. During the first day, the stock price decreased by 44%. During the second day, the stock price increased by 34%. During the third day, the stock price increased by 48%. After the three days, what was the final price of the stock?


    Solution

    Find all the scale factors. Find the product of the inital price and all the scale factors.

    \[4.23\cdot 0.56\cdot1.34\cdot1.48~=~4.7\]

    If you wanted to, you could break this down into three changes.

    \[P_1 ~=~ 0.56\cdot 4.23 ~=~ 2.3688\] \[P_2 ~=~ 1.34\cdot 2.3688 ~=~ 3.174192\] \[P_3 ~=~ 1.48\cdot 3.174192 ~=~ 4.6978042\]

    Now, most stocks are reported rounded to the nearest cent… so these intermediate values might not be entirely realistic… but please do not round the intermediate values when doing this problem.
