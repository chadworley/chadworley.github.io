  1. Question

    What is 4.02% more than 118?


    Solution

    For a “percent more” question, the scale factor is found by dividing the percentage number by 100 and then adding 1.

    Determine that the scale factor equals 1.0402 and multiply it by the given value.

    \[1.0402\cdot 118 ~=~ 122.7436\]
