  1. Question

    An investment earns compound interest with continuous compounding.

    • Principal = \(P=7000\) USD
    • Nominal annual interest rate = \(r~=~0.04 ~=~4\%\)
    • Compounding frequency = \(n\to\infty\) per year
    • Time = \(t=30\) years

    Calculate the amount, in USD, after 30 years by using the continuous compounding formula.

    \[A ~=~ Pe^{rt}\] Euler’s number, \(e\), is approximately 2.7182818. You can round to the nearest dollar. (Tolerance is \(\pm 1\).)


    Solution

    \[A ~=~ 7000 \cdot e^{(0.04)(30)} ~=~ 23240.82\]
