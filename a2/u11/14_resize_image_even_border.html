Question

The inner dimensions of a frame are 38 inches by 16 inches. An artist hopes to print an image with an aspect ratio of 3.85 such that a uniformly wide mat fits around the image.


Find \(y\), the height of the image in inches. The tolerance is \(\pm 0.01\) inches.

Solution

A system of equations can be written from the frames width and height:

\[38 = 3.85y+2b\] \[16 = y+2b\] Subtract the two equations.

\[22 = 2.85y\]

Divide both sides by 2.85.

\[7.7192982 = y\]

To find \(b\), you can rearrange the second equation from the system, and plug in the value of \(y\). \[b ~=~ \frac{16-y}{2} ~=~ \frac{16-7.7192982}{2} ~=~ 4.1403509\]
