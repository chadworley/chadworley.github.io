  1. Question

    Does the complex number \(z=2-6i\) solve the equation below?

    \[z^2-4z+40=0\]


    Solution

    Plug in \(z=2-6i\). Since we are checking if this equation is true, we should probably put a question mark above the equal sign. I didn’t, but keep in mind these equations might be wrong.

    \[(2-6i)^2-4(2-6i)+40=0\]

    Expand the terms. Use FOIL for the squared term.

    \[4-12i-12i+36i^2-8+24i+40=0\]

    Remember \(i^2=-1\). \[4-12i-12i-36-8+24i+40=0\] Combine like terms. \[0+0i=0\] Yay! The complex number satisfies the equation!
