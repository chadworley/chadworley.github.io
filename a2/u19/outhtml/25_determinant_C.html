25_determinant_C


  1. Question

    Given a two-by-two matrix:

    \[M~=~\begin{bmatrix}a & b \\ c & d\end{bmatrix}\]

    The determinant is defined as follows:

    \[\det(M)~=~ad-bc\]

    Let’s practice evaluating the determinant.

    \[Q=\begin{bmatrix}9 & 2 \\ 10 & -6\end{bmatrix}\]

    Evaluate \(\det(Q)\).


    Solution

    \[\det(Q)~=~(9)(-6)-(2)(10)\]

    \[\det(Q)~=~-74\]
