  1. Question

    A linear 2-equation-2-variable system of equations can be expressed generally in standard form, using 6 parameters (\(a\) through \(f\)) and 2 variables (\(x\) and \(y\)).

    \[\begin{align}ax+by &= c \\ dx+ey &=f\end{align}\]

    Using determinants, we can express Cramer’s rule, which gives us the solutions to the system:

    \[x = \frac{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}c & b\\ f & e\end{bmatrix}\right)}{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}a & b\\ d & e\end{bmatrix}\right)}~=~\frac{ce-bf}{ae-bd}\]

    and

    \[y = \frac{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}a & c\\ d & f\end{bmatrix}\right)}{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}a & b\\ d & e\end{bmatrix}\right)}~=~\frac{af-cd}{ae-bd}\]

    Using Cramer’s rule, solve the linear system below:

    \[\begin{align}9x+12y&=-7\\10x+2y&=11\end{align}\]

    Express your answers in decimal form rounded to the hundredths place.

    • \(x=\)
    • \(y=\)


    Solution

    Use Cramer’s rule. Here is the given system:

    \[\begin{align}9x+12y&=-7\\10x+2y&=11\end{align}\]

    Identify the parameters as defined in the general desciption.

    \[a=9\] \[b=12\] \[c=-7\] \[d=10\] \[e=2\] \[f=11\]

    Put parameters into the formula for \(x\).

    \[x = \frac{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}c & b\\ f & e\end{bmatrix}\right)}{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}a & b\\ d & e\end{bmatrix}\right)}~=~\frac{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}-7 & 12\\ 11 & 2\end{bmatrix}\right)}{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}9 & 12\\ 10 & 2\end{bmatrix}\right)}\]

    Remember how to calculate a determinant.

    \[\begin{align}x~&=~\frac{(-7)(2)-(12)(11)}{(9)(2)-(12)(10)}\\\\ x~&=~ \frac{-146}{-102} \\\\ x~&\approx~ 1.43 \end{align}\]

    Put parameters into the formula for \(y\).

    \[y = \frac{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}a & c\\ d & f\end{bmatrix}\right)}{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}a & b\\ d & e\end{bmatrix}\right)}~=~\frac{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}9 & -7\\ 10 & 11\end{bmatrix}\right)}{\det\left(\begin{bmatrix}9 & 12\\ 10 & 2\end{bmatrix}\right)}\]

    \[\begin{align}y~&=~\frac{(9)(11)-(-7)(10)}{(9)(2)-(12)(10)}\\\\ y~&=~ \frac{169}{-102} \\\\ y~&\approx~ 1.70 \end{align}\]
