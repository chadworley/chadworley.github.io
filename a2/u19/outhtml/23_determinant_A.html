  1. Question

    There is actually a shortcut to finding the area of a parallelogram like the one shown below.


    \[A=|x_1y_2-x_2y_1|\]

    With that in mind, find the area of the parallelogram shown below, where each small grey square has an area of 1.


    \[G=~?\]


    Solution

    Identify the coordinates of the vertices.


    • \(x_1 = 6\)
    • \(y_1 = 3\)
    • \(x_2 = 4\)
    • \(y_2 = 7\)

    Use the formula.

    \[G=|6\cdot7-4\cdot3|\] \[G=30\]
