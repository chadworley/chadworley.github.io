14_transpose_matrix


  1. Question

    Let matrix \(M\) be defined below:

    \[M = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 8\\ 1 & 4\\ 2 & 5\\ 3 & 7 \end{bmatrix}\]

    Which matrix equals \(M^\mathrm{T}\), the transpose of \(M\)?

    \[A = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 8\\ 1 & 4\\ 2 & 5\\ 3 & 7 \end{bmatrix}\] \[B = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 1 & 2 & 3\\ 8 & 4 & 5 & 7 \end{bmatrix}\] \[C = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 1\\ 2 & 3\\ 8 & 4\\ 5 & 7 \end{bmatrix}\] \[D = \begin{bmatrix} 6 & 2 & 8 & 5\\ 1 & 3 & 4 & 7 \end{bmatrix}\]


    1. A
    2. B
    3. C
    4. D

    Solution

    You can use SageMath.

    M = matrix([[6,8],
            [1,4],
            [2,5],
            [3,7]])
print(M.transpose())
