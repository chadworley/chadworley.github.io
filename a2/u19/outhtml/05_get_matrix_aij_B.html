    \[A~=~\begin{bmatrix} 17 & 76 & 90 & 71 & 63\\ 69 & 50 & 72 & 33 & 16\\ 42 & 55 & 77 & 52 & 61\\ 68 & 73 & 53 & 99 & 37 \end{bmatrix}\]

    \[a_{3,4}~=~ ?\]

    The element 52 is in row 3 and column 4.
