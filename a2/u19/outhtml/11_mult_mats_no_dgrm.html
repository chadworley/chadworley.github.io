  1. Question

    Let matrices \(A\) and \(B\) be defined below.

    \[A = \begin{bmatrix} 7 & 8 & 3\\ 2 & 6 & 1\\ 9 & 5 & 4 \end{bmatrix}\] \[B = \begin{bmatrix} 9 & 5 & 6\\ 1 & 4 & 7\\ 2 & 8 & 3 \end{bmatrix}\]

    Also, let \(C=AB\) and \(D=BA\).

    • Find element \(d_{2,3}\)

    Solution

    I’d recommend setting up a diagram on (gasp) paper.

    When calculating matrix \(D\) with the matrix multiplication \(D=B\cdot A\), the fact that matrix \(B\) is before matrix \(A\) matters (because matrix multiplication is NOT commutative). So, in our diagram, matrix \(B\) is in bottom-left position and matrix \(A\) is in top-right position.


    Notice we use row 2 from \(B\) and column 3 from \(A\).

    \[d_{2,3}~~=~~1\cdot 3~+~4\cdot 1~+~7\cdot 4\] \[d_{2,3}~~=~~35\]
