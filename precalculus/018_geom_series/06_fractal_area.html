Question


A fractal is generated such that the biggest circle has an area of 1 square units. The next generation has 8 circles, each with an area of \(k=0.05\) square units. In the next generation of 64 circles, each has an area of \(k^2=0.0025\), then 512 circles with area \(k^3\) each, etc…

Find the area of the fractal. The tolerance is \(\pm 0.01\).

Solution

We find the common ratio \(r = (8)(0.05) = 0.4\).

\[1 + 0.4 + 0.4^2 + 0.4^3 + \ldots ~=~ \frac{1}{1-0.4} ~=~ 1.6666667\]
