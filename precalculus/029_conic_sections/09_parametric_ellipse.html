Solution


So: \[\begin{align} h &= -6\\\\ k &= 2\\\\ r_1 &= 3\\\\ r_2 &= 7 \end{align}\]

Substitute those numbers into the parametric system:

\[x=-6+3\cos(t)\] \[y=2+7\sin(t)\]

In Desmos, you’d graph this by typing:

\[(-6+3\cos(t),~2+7\sin(t))\]

and setting bounding \(t\) so that \(0\le t< 2\pi\).
