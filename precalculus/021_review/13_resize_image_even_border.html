Question

The inner dimensions of a frame are 27 inches by 18 inches. An artist hopes to print an image with an aspect ratio of 2.39 such that a uniformly wide mat fits around the image.


Find \(y\), the height of the image in inches. The tolerance is \(\pm 0.01\) inches.

Solution

A system of equations can be written from the frame’s width and height:

\[27 = 2.39y+2b\] \[18 = y+2b\] Subtract the two equations.

\[9 = 1.39y\]

Divide both sides by 1.39.

\[6.4748201 = y\]

To find \(b\), you can rearrange the second equation from the system, and plug in the value of \(y\). \[b ~=~ \frac{18-y}{2} ~=~ \frac{18-6.4748201}{2} ~=~ 5.7625899\]
