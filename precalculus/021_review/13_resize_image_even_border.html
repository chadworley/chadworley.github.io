Question

The inner dimensions of a frame are 34 inches by 12 inches. An artist hopes to print an image with an aspect ratio of 4.36 such that a uniformly wide mat fits around the image.


Find \(y\), the height of the image in inches. The tolerance is \(\pm 0.01\) inches.

Solution

A system of equations can be written from the frame’s width and height:

\[34 = 4.36y+2b\] \[12 = y+2b\] Subtract the two equations.

\[22 = 3.36y\]

Divide both sides by 3.36.

\[6.547619 = y\]

To find \(b\), you can rearrange the second equation from the system, and plug in the value of \(y\). \[b ~=~ \frac{12-y}{2} ~=~ \frac{12-6.547619}{2} ~=~ 2.7261905\]
